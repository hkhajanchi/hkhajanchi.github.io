About

Nov 14 2020

I’m a first year Computer Engineering PhD student at Northwestern University advised by Prof. Simone Campanoni. My research interests are at the intersection of compilers and computer architecture, specifically for resource-constrained environments. I did my undergrad in Electrical Engineering at The College of New Jersey, where I did some research in embedded control and deep learning.

Outside of the lab, I enjoy playing ice hockey, lifting weights, skiing, running and playing tennis. I’ve been an avid reader since I was a kid - huge fan of Dostoevsky and John Steinbeck. Also a recreational video gamer, specifically in Halo and Call of Duty.
